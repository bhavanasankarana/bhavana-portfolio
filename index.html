Hi, I'm Bhavana, a B.Tech student by day and a storyteller by heart. Having navigated the worlds of UI/UX design, Social Media Management, and Published Authorship, I approach every project as a narrative waiting to be told. Whether I am drafting content strategies or building sleek interfaces, my goal is always to create something that resonates, engages, and leaves an impact.
